❓ How to Use ZeroLeak.AI

📁 Step 1: Prepare Your Data

Export your financial data from your accounting system, payment processor, or CRM. Make sure it includes:

  • Date: When the transaction occurred
  • Amount: Transaction value (positive for revenue, negative for refunds)
  • Customer: Customer name or ID
  • Description: What the transaction was for
  • Category: Optional classification

📤 Step 2: Upload Your File

Drag and drop your CSV or Excel file into the upload area, or click to browse and select your file.

🔍 Step 3: AI Analysis

Our AI agents will automatically scan your data for:

  • Duplicate transactions
  • Failed payments
  • Refund patterns
  • Billing anomalies
  • Customer churn indicators

📊 Step 4: Review Results

Get detailed insights with:

  • Visual charts and graphs
  • Priority-ranked issues
  • Actionable recommendations
  • Downloadable reports
